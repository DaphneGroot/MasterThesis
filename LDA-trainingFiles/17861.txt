 VIB FORECASTS UNCHANGED 1996 RESULT. 
 ZEIST, Netherlands, Nov 20 (Reuter) - Property investment fund VIB said on Wednesday it maintained its forecast of an approximately unchanged direct investment result per share for full year 1996. 
 "Based on the results trend in the current fiscal year, VIB maintains its forecast that for full 1996 the direct investment result per share will be at around 1995's level (3.45 guilders), the fund said in a statement.
