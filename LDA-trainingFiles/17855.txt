 POLYNORM H2 1996 PROFITS BELOW H2 1995. 
 AMSTERDAM, Nov 18 (Reuter) - Plastics processor Polynorm NV said on Monday results in the second half of 1996 were recovering, but would still be below results in the second half of 1995. 
 Net profits in the whole of 1995 were 26 million guilders. In the first half of 1995 the firm posted a net profit of 11.4 million guilders. 
 The firm said it expected turnover and profits to rise in 1997.
